What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Hardware failures under normal use
  • Battery defects (not normal degradation)
  • Screen defects (not physical damage)
  • Charging port and button failures

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage (drops, cracks, liquid damage)
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Loss or theft
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Accessories (chargers, cables, cases)
  • Software issues
