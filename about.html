About Sile Communications

Your trusted technology and telecommunications partner in Kitale | Authorized Safaricom Dealer

WHAT WE REALLY DO?

Sile Communications is Kitale's leading independent smartphone and electronics dealer.

We are known mostly for a reputation of providing original smartphones at the most affordable prices any other dealer offers in the market, with a promise of durability, quality and authenticity. Highly spoken of by many whom we have served since our inception in 2010.

As an authorized Safaricom Dealer, we provide all Safaricom services including M-PESA registration, agent registration, ID changes, SIM replacement, Till numbers, Mpesa fraud related cases bundles, and home fiber installation.
