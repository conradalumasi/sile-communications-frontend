Shipping and Return Policy

Delivery Options and Fees

  • Within Kitale CBD: KSh 100 - Delivered within 2-4 hours
  • Outside Kitale (Trans Nzoia County): KSh 400 - Delivered within 24 hours
  • Other Counties (Nationwide): KSh 600 - Delivered within 2-5 business days
  • Shop Pickup: Free - Ready within 1 hour after order confirmation

Free Delivery Offer

Free delivery is available for orders over KSh 10,000 within Kitale CBD and surrounding areas. Offer applies automatically at checkout.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 8AM-6PM; Saturday, 9AM-4PM). Orders placed after 4PM will be processed the next business day.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation via SMS and email with tracking information. You can also track your order status in your account dashboard.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return an item, here's how it works:

  • Return Window: 7 days from date of delivery
  • Condition: Item must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories and tags attached
  • Proof of Purchase: Original receipt or order confirmation required
  • Return Shipping: Customer pays for return shipping unless the item is defective

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened software, games, or digital products
  • Personal care items (earbuds, headphones for hygiene reasons)
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Clearance items (marked as final sale)
  • Screen protectors (once opened)

Defective Items

If you receive a defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost. Defective items are eligible for return within 30 days of purchase.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method. M-PESA refunds will be sent to the registered phone number.

Exchange Policy

Items can be exchanged for a different size, color, or model within 7 days of purchase. Any price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.
